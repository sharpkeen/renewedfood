How It Works

Place partially or even almost fully eaten food in the Renewed Food box, press the start button, and watch it regenerate into a fresh, complete item. Say goodbye to buying extra or bulk quantities! This new innovation also has upgrades coming soon such as Renew Food LASER where the size of your food does not need to be fixed, just point the laser and watch the magic happen. Coming soon also Calorie FREE Renewed Food. The renewed portion of food is no longer counts as calories. Eat freely with no guilt. Buy today!
